Formatting & how it works

Paste a tracklist: one track per line.

  • Common separators: Artist - Title, Artist — Title, Artist: Title, Artist | Title, tabs
  • Numbered or bulleted lists are handled
  • Lines without a separator search as a single phrase

Paste a YouTube playlist URL and click Generate — we fetch the video titles and run them through the parser. Common markers like [Official Video], [Lyrics], [Free DL], and 【...】 tags are stripped automatically.

From 1001tracklists: use the Export Tracklist button for cleanest results. Raw select-all also works — the parser strips:

  • UI noise: Save / Pre-Save buttons, play counts, track numbers, artwork placeholder, [ID] placeholders, repeated uploader usernames
  • Metadata: leading timestamps, w/ overlay markers, trailing [Label] tags, trailing Artwork alt-text
